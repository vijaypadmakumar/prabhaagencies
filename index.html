About us

Prabha Agencies leverages a legacy of over 30 years in Indian distribution. We specialise in launching and managing cosmetic brands across South India. We offer a comprehensive solution for brands seeking to establish a strong presence in this region. Our approach combines a distribution network with a skilled marketing team, all fuelled by a deep understanding of the evolving beauty landscape in India. We work alongside with our affiliate V4 Associates and our sister company Prabha Global Trade. V4 Associates primarily focus on wholesale and retail sector whereas Prabha Global Trade focus on import-activities.

Our Vision

Prabha Agencies aspires to become a preeminent and well-respected FMCG distribution company within India, specializing in the cosmetics, skincare, and alcoholic beverage industries. We aim to establish ourselves as the preferred partner for brands and manufacturers seeking to enter or expand their market presence within India. We are committed to delivering exceptional customer service, fostering a robust and adaptable distribution network, and optimizing all facets of the distribution process.
