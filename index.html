About us

Prabha Agencies leverages a legacy of over 30 years in Indian distribution. We specialise in launching and managing cosmetic brands across South India. We offer a comprehensive solution for brands seeking to establish a strong presence in this region. Our approach combines a distribution network with a skilled marketing team, all fuelled by a deep understanding of the evolving beauty landscape in India.

Our Vision

Prabha Agencies envisions itself as a top FMCG distributor in India, specialising in cosmetics, skincare, and alcoholic beverages. We strive to be the go-to partner for brands looking to enter or expand their reach within the country. By providing exceptional customer service and building a strong, adaptable distribution network, we aim to optimise every step of the distribution process.

Associated Firms

Prabha Global Trade

Prabha Global Trade is the import and national distribution arm behind the success of Prabha Agencies. We source top-tier products from around the world, ensuring consistent availability and high quality. We distribute these products to wholesalers and retailers across the country, making them accessible to businesses of all sizes. By working together, V4 Associates, Prabha Global Trade and Prabha Agencies provide a seamless experience for our clients, from carefully curated products to reliable distribution.

V4 Associates

V4 Associates is your one-stop shop for all things beauty! We cater directly to retail stores and salons, offering a wide range of high-quality products to elevate your business. Our knowledgeable sales team is here to assist you in finding the perfect products to suit your specific needs and answer any questions you may have.

We have our direct presence in Mumbai, Bangalore and Kochi.

What We Offer

Warehousing and distribution
  • Optimised Warehousing: Leveraging advanced systems and strategic storage solutions, we ensure efficient product placement, streamlined picking & packing, and real-time inventory visibility to maximize fulfillment speed and accuracy.
  • Scalable Distribution Network: Our adaptable and geographically diverse warehousing infrastructure allows for flexible order fulfillment, ensuring efficient delivery across your target markets, regardless of growth or seasonal fluctuations.
Market entry facilitation
  • Seamless Market Entry: Our experienced market entry team streamlines registration, leveraging established partner networks.

Streamlined logistics
  • Extensive Logistics Network: Prabha Agencies leverages a robust logistics network for efficient product movement. Strategically positioned warehousing facilities in Mumbai, Bangalore, and Kochi ensure optimal product availability.
  • Streamlined Supply Chain Management: Our experienced team manages all aspects of product import, warehousing, and distribution across our designated sales channels. This ensures timely delivery and product accessibility for customers.
Dedicated customer support
  • Empowering Informed Decisions: Responsive email support, user-friendly websites, and interactive features like Instagram chats provide easy access to information, fostering trust and informed purchases.

  • Expert Support: Our highly trained team is equipped to address all inquiries promptly and professionally.
D2C Platform
  • Building and managing your own online platform: Our team creates a user-friendly platform that facilitates purchases and provides valuable content to engage customers.
  • Boosting brand presence on major marketplaces: We establish your brand on popular e-commerce sites like Amazon India and Flipkart, expanding your reach.
  • Fulfilment and promotion: We ensure smooth order fulfilment and actively work to drive sales for your brand on all platforms.
Building offline presence
  • Expanded Market Reach: Complement your online presence with targeted product placement within our network of reputable salons and beauty shops.
